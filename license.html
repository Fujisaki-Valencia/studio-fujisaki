License & Credits

The original paintings

Every artwork featured on Studio Fujisaki is a reproduction of a work that is in the public domain. Copyright on these paintings has expired, and they are free for anyone to use. Where a museum provides the digitised image, we follow its open-access terms and credit the institution.

The wallpapers

  • Free to download. All wallpapers on this site are free.
  • Personal use. You may use them as desktop and phone wallpapers, and on your personal devices, at no cost.
  • No resale. Please don't resell the files or claim them as your own work. They are meant to be shared, not sold.
  • No attribution required for personal use, though a link back is always appreciated.

Credits & sourcing policy

On each wallpaper's page we list the artist, the era, and the museum or collection that holds the original. We deliberately keep this credit on the page and never burn text onto the image, so the wallpaper stays clean on your screen while the attribution remains transparent and easy to find.

If you believe an artwork has been mis-attributed or should not be listed as public domain, please get in touch and we will review it promptly.
