Credits

Where a wallpaper has a known maker or source, we list it on that wallpaper's page — the artist, era, or origin, as far as it's known. We deliberately keep this credit on the page and never burn text onto the image, so the wallpaper stays clean on your screen while the credit remains easy to find.

If you're a rights holder and believe something here shouldn't be, or is credited incorrectly, please get in touch and we'll review it promptly.
