Last updated: —

Studio Fujisaki (“we”, “us”) operates this website. This page explains what information is collected when you visit and how it is used. This is a template you should review and adapt before relying on it.

Cookies

At present the site does not set its own tracking cookies. If cookies are introduced (for example through analytics or advertising), this section will list each cookie, its purpose, and its lifetime.

Analytics

If web analytics are enabled, they may collect anonymised data such as pages visited, approximate location, browser type, and referring site, to help us understand which wallpapers are popular. No personally identifying information is intentionally collected.

Advertising (Google AdSense)

We may in the future display ads served by Google AdSense. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. For more information, see Google's policies on the use of cookies in advertising.

Downloads & external storage

Full-size wallpaper downloads are hosted on external object storage. Requesting a download may expose standard technical information (such as your IP address) to that storage provider, as with any web request.

Contact

For any privacy questions, please contact us via the address listed on the site.
